Why you'll love Risk Assessments

Proactive compliance that protects your people and your business

Guided risk templates

Pre-built templates for common electrical safety scenarios. Follow structured workflows to ensure nothing is missed.

Comprehensive hazard library

Access a database of common electrical hazards with suggested controls. Based on DGUV and VDE standards.

Risk matrix calculation

Automatically calculate risk scores based on likelihood and severity. Visual matrix makes prioritisation easy.

Control measure tracking

Document control measures and track their implementation. Ensure recommended actions are actually completed.

Test interval definition

Set inspection frequencies based on risk levels. High-risk assets get more attention, low-risk assets get less.

Audit documentation

Generate compliance documentation that auditors love. Prove due diligence with clear risk assessment records.

Visual risk prioritisation

The risk matrix combines likelihood and severity to calculate an overall risk score. This helps you focus resources where they matter most.

  • Low risk – Standard inspection intervals
  • Medium risk – Increased monitoring
  • High risk – Immediate action required
