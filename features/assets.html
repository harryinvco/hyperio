Benefits

Complete control over your assets

Your complete electrical infrastructure at your fingertips, organised exactly how you need it.

Hierarchical location mapping

Organise assets by building, floor, room, or any structure that matches your facility. Navigate your entire portfolio effortlessly.

QR code asset tagging

Generate and print QR codes for every asset. Technicians can scan to instantly access inspection history and documentation.

Document storage per asset

Attach manuals, certificates, and photos directly to each asset. Everything is in one place when you need it.

Lifecycle tracking

Track asset age, maintenance history, and replacement schedules. Plan capital expenditure with confidence.

Powerful search

Find any asset instantly by name, serial number, location, or custom fields. No more hunting through files.
