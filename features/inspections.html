Three ways to manage inspections

Whether you use OMS technicians, your own team, or both - Inspektra has you covered.

Automatic

OMS Inspections

Reports from OMS Prufservice appear automatically in Inspektra. No upload, no data entry, no errors.

  • Real-time sync with OMS
  • Full inspection reports
  • Defect photos included
Flexible

In-house Inspections

Conduct your own inspections using customisable templates. Perfect for visual checks, routine maintenance, and internal audits.

  • Custom inspection templates
  • Mobile app for field work
  • Photo documentation
  • Offline capability
Smart

Inspection Scheduling

Plan ahead with intelligent scheduling. Set recurring inspections, receive reminders, and never miss a compliance deadline.

  • Recurring schedules
  • Email & in-app reminders

OMS results when they're shared

Receive inspection results, reports, and certificates directly from OMS without manual uploads or email handling.
