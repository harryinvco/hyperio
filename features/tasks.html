Why you'll love Task Management

From defect to resolution, all in one system

Auto-create from defects

When an inspection finds a defect, a task is automatically created. No manual work, no missed issues.

Assign to users or roles

Assign tasks to specific team members, contractors, or roles. The right person gets notified instantly.

Due date reminders

Set due dates and receive automatic reminders. Never let a critical task slip through the cracks.

Resolution verification

When a task is marked complete, require photo evidence or verification. Prove that issues were actually fixed.

Priority levels

Set priority levels based on risk. Critical safety issues get flagged and escalated appropriately.

Comments & history

Add comments, attach files, and track the full history. Everyone stays informed as tasks progress.
