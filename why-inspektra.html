Our Journey

From field work to digital innovation

Every great product starts with a real problem. Here's how we got here.

2008

The Beginning

OMS Prufservice was founded with a vision to modernize electrical safety inspections across Germany.

2015

Digital Transformation

After conducting over 5 million inspections, we identified critical inefficiencies in compliance management.

2019

Inspektra is Born

We launched Inspektra, combining our field expertise with cutting-edge technology to solve compliance challenges.

2023

Industry Recognition

Reached 500+ enterprise clients and crossed 12 million managed inspections across the DACH region.

Today

Leading the Industry

Continuing to innovate with AI-powered insights, predictive maintenance, and seamless integrations.

Our Values

What drives us every day

These core principles guide every decision we make and every feature we build.

Safety First

We believe electrical safety is a fundamental right, not just a compliance checkbox. Every feature we build protects people and property.

Excellence

Good enough isn't good enough. We strive for excellence in every line of code, every customer interaction, and every inspection report.

Transparency

We build trust through clarity. Our platform provides unalterable audit trails and clear visibility, so you're always audit-ready.

Customer Focus

Innovation
