# Electrical Safety Checklist

Follow these best practices to ensure a safe working environment.

## Daily Checks

- [ ] Check for frayed cords or exposed wires.
- [ ] Ensure all panels are accessible (3 feet clearance).
- [ ] Verify that lockout/tagout kits are available.

## Monthly Inspections

- [ ] Test GFCI outlets.
- [ ] Inspect portable power tools.
- [ ] Review safety logs.

## Do's and Don'ts

| Do | Don't |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Do** treat all circuits as live until tested. | **Don't** overload outlets. |
| **Do** use proper PPE. | **Don't** use metal ladders near power lines. |
