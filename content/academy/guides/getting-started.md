# Getting Started with Inspektra

Welcome to Inspektra! This guide will walk you through the initial setup of your workspace.

## 1. Setting up your Organization

Navigate to the **Settings** tab and select **Organization**. Here you can:
*   Upload your logo
*   Set your timezone
*   Define user roles

## 2. Inviting Team Members

Click on **Users** and then **Invite User**. Enter their email address and assign a role.

> **Note:** Admin users have full access to all settings.

## 3. Creating your first Site

A "Site" represents a physical location where inspections occur. Go to **Sites** > **Add Site**.
