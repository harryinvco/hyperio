# How to Conduct a Risk Assessment

Inspektra's AI-powered risk assessment tool helps you identify potential hazards quickly.

## Step 1: Start a New Assessment

From the dashboard, click **New Assessment**.

## Step 2: Select the Area

Choose the specific area or equipment you are inspecting.

## Step 3: Capture Images

Use the mobile app to take photos of the equipment. Our AI will analyze them for visible defects.

```javascript
// Example of API usage for developers
const assessment = await inspektra.createAssessment({
  siteId: 'site_123',
  type: 'electrical'
});
```

## Step 4: Review AI Suggestions

The system will highlight potential risks. Confirm or reject these suggestions.
