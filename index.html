How Inspektra Works

A clear path from risk assessment to completed inspections.

1

Define risks and test intervals

Set up your asset groups, hazards, and testing intervals based on your internal requirements.

2

Receive OMS inspection results

OMS Prüfservice conducts professional on-site inspections. Results appear in Inspektra automatically.

3

Perform your own tests

Carry out in-house inspections such as PAT testing, new device checks, and home-office testing.

4

Review findings and take action

Incidents automatically become tasks. Assign, resolve, and update each asset's safety status.

5

Stay compliant across all sites

Track deadlines, risks, and open items on the dashboard. Keep every asset up to date.
